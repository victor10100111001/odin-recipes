Chicken enchiladas

Description

This recipe will teach you to make the most delicious chicken enchiladas

Ingredients:

  • Olive oil
  • Boneless chicken
  • Onion
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Dried parsley
  • Oregano
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic
  • Chili powder
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Flour tortillas
  • Taco sauce

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Heat the oil
  3. Transfer chicken to cutting board
  4. Add ingredients
  5. Make it look nice
  6. Bake in the preheated oven
